General Information

I am Alicia Cole, I have been an artist and a teacher for 14 years. I have an impressionistic style, and I work in all mediums! My gandmother was a professional potrait artist, and her passion for art has Inspired me and my career in art. My recent paintings include encaustic paintings on wooden panels, reverse paintings on plexi glass, mixed media potraits and mexied media abstracts. I teach private art lessons and paint commissioned art
